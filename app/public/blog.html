Working Title

A Compilation of My Adventures, Mishaps, and Advice I received throughout my career transition into Tech

If you're reading this, you probably understand that transitioning into a tech career from a different field is HARD (or you'll probably understand that soon). This blog started as my way of pulling together all my realizations, misadventures, and amazing advice I've received from industry professionals. My hope now is that by publishing some of the wisdom I've gained (with permission, of course), it may help someone else in their career transition.

I graduated with a STEM degree, and from there I tried on many different roles to find my fit before I even knew what software engineering was. Now, as a coding bootcamp graduate, I'm seeking a job. It's a challenging process. There are literally listings for jobs requiring more years of experience with a framework than the framework's existed for. Add that to rigorous 4-5 step interviews and the ever-changing landscape of LinkedIn guidelines, and you've got your work cut out for you. To me, the part that's always felt easy (and is by far my favorite) has been getting to meet people and listen to their stories and advice. Everyone I've met has been incredibly generous with their time and wisdom, and now I'm thrilled to share that with you, reader. So let's dive in!
